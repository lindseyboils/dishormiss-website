Privacy Policy

Dish or Miss
Effective Date: March 5, 2026

Welcome to Dish or Miss! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Dish or Miss is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and store the following information locally on your device only:

  • Restaurant visits: Names, addresses, and details of restaurants you log
  • Dish information: Names, photos, ratings, and notes about dishes you've tried
  • Your preferences: Dietary restrictions, cuisine preferences, and saved wishlist items
  • Location data: Used only to find nearby restaurants (with your permission). Location data is not stored or transmitted.

How We Use Your Information

All data you enter into Dish or Miss is stored locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage). We use this data to:

  • Display your restaurant history and dish ratings
  • Provide personalized "For You" recommendations based on your preferences
  • Help you find nearby restaurants using your location (when permitted)
  • Remember your dietary restrictions and cuisine preferences

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: Currently, Dish or Miss does not use cloud servers or remote databases. All your data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Photos: Any photos you take or select are stored locally on your device within the app's secure storage.

Future Cloud Sync: If we introduce optional cloud backup in the future, we will update this policy and request your explicit consent before enabling any data synchronization.

Third-Party Services

Dish or Miss integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Places API: Used to search for restaurants and retrieve restaurant details (name, address, hours, photos, ratings). Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Location Services: We request your device's location permission to show nearby restaurants. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

We do not use analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools. We do not collect any personal information beyond what you voluntarily enter into the app.

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is viewable within the app at any time
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any restaurant, dish, or preference at any time
  • Delete: You can delete all your data by using the "Delete All Data" option in the app's Profile settings
  • Location: You can enable or disable location permissions in your device settings

Children's Privacy

Dish or Miss is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and notify you within the app. Your continued use of Dish or Miss after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it remains there until you manually delete it or uninstall the app. Uninstalling Dish or Miss will permanently delete all your data from your device.

International Users

Dish or Miss can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information is not transferred across borders. The Google Places API may process requests according to Google's privacy practices.
